Screen Notes — ‘Transaction Screen:’

This will be the ‘main screen’ is what all the searches are looking for. In the upper left is the ‘Guest Info’ and can be exactly the same as on the ‘name search’ screen.

The reservation information, particular to that stay, will be at the bottom left and require some thinking on how we handle guests who move from room to room during their stay. Eg: They arrive on August 5th for Lila’s cabin and depart Aug. 15th, but move to a Beach Cabin on the 9th and a lodge room on the 12th, etc. A pull-down menu of room types should have a brief description of the bed layout.

On this screen, as opposed to the ‘guest notes’ field there may be buttons (or a pull down menu, I’m sure you’ll have all the good ideas) that is used to populate the fields that are printed to the various reports. Diet information (eg: vegetarian, fish ok, etc.) would go to the Kitchen report, ‘need split beds and a cot’ would go to Housekeeping etc.

‘Re-book’ would open another transaction screen with only the guest info printed and have the user input the new reservation dates and room, along with any deposit on the actual ‘transaction’ portion of the screen. ‘Cancel’ would move some information to the ‘cancellation’ report. There will have to be some detail work done on how deposits are refunded or transferred to another reservation, we would anticipate a ‘transfer’ or ‘refund’ item selectable as a transaction type with the amount noted.

In keeping with the motto of ‘dumb it down’ we would like to keep the transaction portion of this screen as simple as is needed to feed the Daily Cash report. We are envisioning deposits and payments as negative numbers and charges (for rooms, sundries, drinks, etc.) as positive numbers so a payment, equal to all the charges will bring a zero balance at the bottom. Since the deposit is a negative number when applied, it is already deducted.

All inputs on this portion will probably need dates and ‘type codes’ to get things compiled and sent to the proper reports, but you will have a way better idea of the nuts and bolts, so I’ll stick mainly to what we’d like and you can let me know how, or if, it’s possible.

How this screen works with the actual Daily Cash Activity Report (‘DCAR’ or ‘Cash Sheet’) will definitely take up a good portion of our work on this project. The DCAR is something that we would like to resemble the existing one since it feeds into our already established revenue summary and bank deposit sheets and systems.

As a side note, we would actually like all of our reports and folios to be as close as possible to our existing ones. The part of this system that needs simplifying is how the user interacts with the guest and reservations screens.
