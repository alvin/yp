> I need to pull up stays instantly and make sure the day’s cash and reports balance exactly as printed.

You oversee front-desk operations and ensure reservations, notes, and transactions feed the printed reports correctly. You want quick lookups, calm workflows, and transparent reconciliation so you can confidently sign off on daily cash.

## Goals

- Find the right reservation in seconds by name or number
- Verify deposits, charges, and payments trace cleanly to DCAR
- Get tomorrow’s print batch out on time with minimal fuss
- Resolve exceptions without disrupting the paper workflow

## Frustrations

- Having to chase details across multiple places or reports
- Ambiguous reservation status or unclear note ownership
- Reprinting the wrong document because selections weren’t obvious
- Daily cash that doesn’t reconcile after manual adjustments

## A day in the life

You start with the lookup screen, searching by name, reservation number, or date. You open the transaction screen when needed, then review and reconcile the Daily Cash Activity Report and its appendix reports so the printed workflow matches what you expect.
