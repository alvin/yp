> I need housekeeping and in-house lists to reflect today’s status and stay notes accurately.

You manage operational readiness using housekeeping, in-house, and manual sales list outputs. Your goal is to make sure statuses, note dates, and instructions are present for the selected day so operations can run without guessing.

## Goals

- Print housekeeping reports for a chosen date
- Print in-house lists for current occupancy statuses
- Use kitchen and manual sales outputs as needed
- Ensure room statuses and note dates are visible

## Frustrations

- Statuses missing or not grouped the way staff expect
- Occupancy notes not tied to the correct reservation/room move
- Housekeeping instructions not present or unclear
- Manual sales lists not preserving room order and cancelled handling

## Morning readiness checks

You rely on the operational report outputs that preserve statuses like Arrive Today, Depart Today, In House, and Move In. You also need note dates and housekeeping instructions to show exactly for the selected day.

## Keeping report format dependable

You expect in-house and manual sales list outputs to preserve grouping, counts, and room ordering conventions so staff can act on the same format every day.
