> I enter and correct reservation details and ledger lines so the printed folios and bills come out right.

You support bookings and day-to-day front desk entry. Your focus is entering accurate reservation details, recording room moves, and posting deposits/payments and charges so documents and DCAR reflect reality.

## Goals

- Create new reservations correctly within the one-year window
- Record room assignments and room moves during a stay
- Post deposits, prepayments, and payments with correct categories
- Override prices when staff price changes are needed

## Frustrations

- Accidentally entering a reservation outside the allowed booking horizon
- Forgetting to record a move-date and losing reporting history
- Mis-posting a deposit vs prepayment category
- Having to re-do transaction lines because the output looked wrong

## Responsibilities

You build new reservations from the lookup screen, confirm dates and occupancy, and maintain room/guest linkages. When the stay progresses you update moves and ledger activity so printing (confirmation, folio, bill, cancellation notice) is supported without surprises.
